This e-commerce sales dashboard provides valuable insights into the business performance and customer behavior. By analyzing various metrics, the dashboard helps the business identify key areas for improvement and opportunities for growth.

The dashboard reveals that the total sales amount is 161K, with a profit of 26K. The sum of quantity sold is 2008, and the average order value (AOV) is 44K. These figures indicate the overall business health and can be used to set future targets.

The "Profit by Sub-Category" chart shows that certain regions, such as Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh, contribute significantly to the profits, while others might need attention to boost sales.

The "Sum of Quantity by Payment Mode" pie chart highlights that Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most preferred payment method, accounting for 43.58% of the total transactions. This insight can be used to ensure that COD services are optimized and that alternative payment methods are encouraged.
